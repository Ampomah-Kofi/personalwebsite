Active Projects

Building privacy-preserving, secure, and trustworthy AI systems — with a focus on understanding and defending against real-world threats.

🤖

BotLens

Privacy Auditing for Third-Party Community Bots

A privacy auditing framework that analyzes how third-party community bots on Discord, Reddit, and Slack collect and expose user data — surfacing hidden risks and informing platform governance.

  • Automated auditing pipeline for bot data-collection behavior
  • Analysis of permission scopes and data-access patterns
  • Frameworks for communicating privacy risks to non-expert users
Usable Privacy Bot Ecosystems Security Auditing Social Platforms
🧠

Adversarial Transferability in Medical Imaging

Beyond Attack Success Rate

Investigating adversarial transferability — adversarial examples crafted against one model deceiving another — in medical imaging AI, where cross-model vulnerabilities could directly impact patient safety.

  • Multi-metric evaluation framework beyond simple attack success rate
  • Analysis of transferability patterns across model architectures
  • Robustness standards for trustworthy medical AI deployment
Adversarial ML Medical AI Trustworthy AI Robustness
